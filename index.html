Controls

To start/stop the timer, you can click the timer or use ENTER/SPACE.

How to Play

Get as close as you can to one second. The timer automatically stops at two seconds.
The timer is organized in this order:

minutes : seconds : milliseconds

You can check the controls for how to start/stop the timer. You can also see
your high score next to the controls. The score once you stop the timer is your
current score.

Score

Depending on your score's postivity or negativity, your score does not differ in any way.
Your score is calculated using a simple function. If your score was under 1 second, the formula would be
score = 1000 + -milliseconds. If your score was over one second, the formula would be score = milliseconds - 1000.
