To start/stop the timer, you can click the timer or use ENTER/SPACE.

How to Play

Get as close as you can to one second. The timer is organized in this order:

min:sec:millisecond

You can check the controls for how to start/stop the timer. You can also see
your high score below the title 'Second Game'.
